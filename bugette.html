Brand Designer

Bugette Cricket Flour & Protein

CONCEPT | Branding | Logo & Submarks | Package Design | Brand Touchpoints | Landing Page

The other cricket products I've seen on the market are “sciencey” which isn't bad, but I was aiming for more approachable. I wanted to take cricket flour and wrap it in European nostalgia while also promoting its sustainable qualities. I developed a brand of cricket flour that would fit in but stand out on the shelves. It's cozy, it's yummy, it's fresh baked goods on a mission.
