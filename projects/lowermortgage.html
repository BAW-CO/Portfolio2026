Lower Mortgage | Nationwide FinTech Marketing

Senior Graphic Designer

GOAL: Lower's marketing team needed to execute high-volume campaigns that supported both corporate B2B initiatives and consumer-facing efforts for retail mortgage branches. Campaigns required consistent branding, compliance approval, and fast production timelines across multiple channels.

ROLE: I owned marketing initiatives from concept through execution, ensuring campaigns were delivered on time while maintaining brand consistency and regulatory accuracy. My work supported the company's most successful business quarter to date.

I also designed landing pages and maintained campaign content within our CMS, ensuring marketing launches ran smoothly across digital platforms.

OUTCOME: These integrated campaigns helped support a record-performing business quarter for Lower while maintaining consistent brand standards across both B2B and consumer-facing marketing channels.
