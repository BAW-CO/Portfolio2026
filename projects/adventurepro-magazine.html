Adventure Pro Magazine

Layout + Ad Design

At Ad Pro I worked with our writers and editor to produce this magazine from start to finish every quarter. Our mission was to showcase the natural beauty of the four corners region through every season, tell the tales of our local legends, keep readers informed of environmental issues and policies that effect them, and show that everyone can be an adventurer (not just the pros.)

I laid out the publication for print, photo-editing, ad design, file management, generated social media and digital ads, and communicated with our printer to ensure a successful hand off.
