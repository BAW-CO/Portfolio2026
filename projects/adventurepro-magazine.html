Adventure Pro Magazine

Layout + Ad Design

GOAL: Adventure Pro publishes a quarterly magazine dedicated to the landscapes, people, and outdoor culture of the Four Corners region. Each issue needed to balance striking photography, compelling storytelling, and advertising content while maintaining a cohesive visual identity and meeting tight print production deadlines.

ROLE: I collaborated with the editorial team to produce the magazine from concept through final print production each quarter. Working alongside writers and editors, I was responsible for shaping the visual presentation of the publication and ensuring the design supported both the editorial voice and the reader experience.

PROCESS: I designed and laid out the magazine for print, developing page structures that highlighted photography while keeping long-form stories clear and engaging to read. My role also included photo editing, advertisement design, file management, and preparing all files for print production. Beyond the print publication, I created supporting marketing materials including social media graphics and digital ads to help promote each issue. I also worked directly with the printer to ensure all files were properly prepared and delivered, managing the final handoff to keep production running smoothly.

OUTCOME: Each quarterly issue successfully brought together regional storytelling, environmental awareness, and outdoor adventure into a cohesive publication. Through careful layout, image editing, and production management, the magazine delivered a polished print product that celebrated the culture and natural beauty of the Four Corners region.
