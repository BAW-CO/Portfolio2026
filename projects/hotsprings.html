Durango Hot Springs Resort & Spa

Branding, Social Media Templates, Web Design, Ad Design

The Durango Hot Springs prides themselves first and foremost on their healing water and secondly on the peaceful environment they provide. When they finished a massive remodel of the grounds and facilities they opted for a branding facelift as well. They wanted to refresh the logo, typography, colors, and marketing materials. They wanted to firmly establish themselves as the place to be after a day of skiing and show off their new spa offerings. This was a project of mine while I workd for BCI Media, a marketing firm in southwest Colorado.

I was lead designer on this project and I worked with a photographer on shoots, a developer on the website design implementation, social media coordinators on a posting schedule, and a head of marketing on an advertising strategy. Together we surpassed our client's goal of selling 150 annual passes and 150 punch passes, with 224 annual passes and 187 punch passes after the first six-months of our rebrand launch.
